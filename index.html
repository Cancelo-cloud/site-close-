DUCT

Our rivers are calling for our attention. Across South Africa, we observe a concerning decline in water quality and availability. DUCT is actively working across urban and rural landscapes, implementing holistic programs that promote sustainable livelihoods for communities while engaging in vital work for the common good. Our goal is to co-create a future where rivers flourish as vibrant ecosystems, supporting biodiversity and the well-being of those who rely on them.

Who We Are
What We Do

With endless environmental challenges, and an overwhelming unemployment crisis in South Africa, DUCT’s implementation model centres on inspiring and up-skilling individuals to make a difference in their neighbourhoods.

To champion the health of the Umgeni, Msunduzi rivers & beyond.

OUR MISSION

At DUCT, we champion collaborative, community-driven approaches to river rehabilitation, water quality management and environmental conservation.

OUR VISION

An ecologically healthy and biologically diverse Umgeni - Msunduzi river system that provides sustained ecological goods and services for the communities that depend on them for survival.

OUR APPROACH

  • Community-led initiatives and people-centred solutions,
  • Blended finance, multi-party programmes for maximum impact,
  • Collaboration and capacity building with local organizations,
  • Comprehensive catchment management and citizen science tools,
  • Practical occupation pathways for environmental workers.

OUR VALUES

  • Environmental sustainability and conservation,
  • Community empowerment and participation,
  • Innovation and adaptability,
  • Collaboration and partnership.

IMPACT GOALS

  • Improved river health and biodiversity.
  • Increased environmental awareness and stewardship.
  • Support for local economies and sustainable livelihood.

Get Involved

We adopt a comprehensive approach to improving river health and promoting environmental sustainability, focusing on community-led initiatives and people-centred solutions steered through the model of “EnviroChamps."

EnviroChamps

An Enviro Champ is a person who works for the common good in their area. They're community-spirited, seeking to care for and uplift their local communities. EnviroChamps facilitate environmental education, forging vital relationships within their neighbourhoods.
